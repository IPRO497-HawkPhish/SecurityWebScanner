Welcome to HawkPhish

What is HawkPhish?

HawkPhish is a Google Chrome extension that helps you stay safe from malicious and phishing websites. Every time you enter a new site, HawkPhish's algortithm determines if a website is safe, and lets you know!

What Makes HawkPhish Different?

HawkPhish gives a security star rating based on the website's security.

What Does HawkPhish Look For?

HawkPhish examines a website's URL, domain extension, certificates, amongst other security factors to ensure the sites you visit are taking the proper cybersecurity protocols, so you don't have to!
